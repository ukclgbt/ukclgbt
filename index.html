University of Kent LGBT+ Society

The University of Kent Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgender Society is a social society with the aim of providing a safe space for those who identify as part of the LGBT umbrella.

We welcome people of all sexualities and genders; there is no limit.

Find us on Facebook Our Kent Union Page

The UKC LGBT Society exists to represent, inspire, and foster a healthy community for the LGBT community at Kent.

About Us

The University of Kent LGBT+ Society is run by a group of elected committee, all of whom are students at the university. The committee are all unpaid volunteers who give up their free time for the society. There is a huge variety of members but it will be hard to meet everyone at once so please keep coming and meet us all!

We run daytime and night time events on campus weekly along with off campus events throughout the term including trips to London and Brighton. By having paid members, we get money per person from Kent Union to go towards making events, activities and trips for your benefit. If you'd like to give us your ideas for society events then feel free to email us or speak to one of us at an event.

You can find out about events and hear more from the society firstly in our Facebook Group but also on our Facebook Page, Twitter, Instagram, Kent Union page and of course on Snapchat (@ukclgbt)!

Press Guidelines & Resources

If you wish to refer to the University of Kent LGBT+ Society then please abide by the following simple rules.

  • Refer to the society only by 'University of Kent LGBT+ Society' or 'UKC LGBT+ Society'.
  • Do not publish images or names of any member of the society without prior written permission. This restriction includes the committee members.

If you mention our society in your publication/article then please do let us know so we can include it on our website!
