University of Kent LGBT Society

The University of Kent Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgender Society is a social society with the aim of providing a safe space for those who identify as part of the LGBT umbrella.

We welcome people of all sexualities and genders; there is no limit.

Find us on Facebook Our Kent Union Page

The UKC LGBT Society exists to represent, inspire, and foster a healthy community for the LGBT community at Kent.
